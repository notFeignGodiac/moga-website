Frequently Asked Questions

Is MOGA a registered company? ▼
Yes. MOGA is a fully registered entity with the CIPC (Companies and Intellectual Property Commission) in South Africa. All our documentation is available on request.
How do you handle client data? ▼
All client data is handled in strict accordance with POPIA and GDPR standards. We implement encryption at rest and in transit, strict access controls, and regular security audits. Data is never shared with third parties without explicit written consent.
Do you carry professional indemnity insurance? ▼
Yes. MOGA carries professional indemnity insurance covering all software development and consulting engagements. Certificates of insurance are available to clients upon request before project commencement.
What are your contract and payment terms? ▼
All engagements are governed by a formal service agreement. Payment terms are milestone-based for projects and monthly for retainer services. We accept EFT, card, and cryptocurrency.
